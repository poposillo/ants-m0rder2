I kill ants with bottom glass

How do I do this?

Common question among beginners. I get it — we're all kids at heart. I've brought you three ways to do it.

To-do list:

  • Find a bottle (it has to be clear).
  • Mark the bottom with a glass cutter.
  • Run it under hot water and break the glass where it was marked.

Now that you’ve done all this, go find an ant colony and place the bottom glass under the sun until the sunlight coming through forms a small, focused circle of light. That’s enough to incinerate an ant — or even paper, and many other bugs.
